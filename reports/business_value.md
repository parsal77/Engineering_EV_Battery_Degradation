# Business Value

## EV and Fleet Impact
- Better RUL estimation supports proactive maintenance scheduling.
- SOH tracking improves residual-value and warranty risk estimation.
- Reliable battery health prediction reduces range-anxiety uncertainty.

## Operational Benefits
- Prioritize high-risk packs before field failures.
- Improve service planning and parts inventory forecasting.
- Enable data-driven battery lifecycle policies.

## Product Path
The project artifacts can be integrated into a lightweight monitoring app (Streamlit prototype) and later migrated into production BMS analytics stacks.
